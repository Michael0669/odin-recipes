Chicken Nuggets

Description:

Here's how to make some of the crispiest chicken nuggets in the world! By following the ingredient list below and following the cooking instructions, you too can have nuggets just like the picture above.

The herbs and spices that mix together with the chicken will have you wishing you could have made more! In just a few simple ingredients, you'll find yourself enjoying these nuggets in no time! It is a quick, tasty family meal that will have your kids begging for more each night! Enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • Vegetable Oil For Frying
  • 4 Cups of Fresh Flower
  • 6 Tablespoons of Ground Black Pepper
  • 4 Large Eggs, Beaten
  • 8 Skinless, Boneless Chicken Breast

Directions:

  1. Heat 1 inch oil in a large skillet or saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Stir together flour, garlic salt, and pepper in a bowl. Dip chicken pieces into beaten eggs, then press each piece into flour mixture to coat well; shake off excess flour. Place coated chicken pieces onto a plate.
  3. Working in batches, fry chicken in hot oil until golden brown and no longer pink in the center. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).

The exact amount will vary depending on cooking time and temperature, ingredient density, and the specific type of oil used.
