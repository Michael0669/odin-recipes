Pizza

Pizza Description

BOLD, medium sized pizza containing all of the ingredients that you choose! Feel free to add anything to your pizza! Following the directions below will allow you to create the best pizza you have ever cooked in your life!

Following the recipe below uses the freshest ingredients from around the world! By doing this it allows your pizza to become more flavorful than the traditional pizza! If you choose to use local ingredients from the super market, this may lead to results that are less than satisfactory!

ingredients:

  • 3 Cups Fresh Flower
  • 2 Cups Filtered Water
  • 5 Fresh Tomatoes
  • 1 Package Fresh Cut Pepparoni
  • 1/4 Stick Organic Butter
  • Fresh Basil
  • Salt/Pepper

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Lightly grease a pizza pan.
  2. Place warm water in a bowl; add yeast and sugar. Mix and let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes.
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture; beat until smooth. You can do this by hand or use a stand mixer fitted with a dough hook to make it easier. Let rest for 5 minutes.
  4. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a 12-inch circle. Transfer to the prepared pizza pan.
  5. Spread crust with sauce and toppings of your choice.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes. Remove from the oven and let cool for 5 minutes before serving.
